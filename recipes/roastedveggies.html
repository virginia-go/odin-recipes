Roasted Veggies

  • Ingredients
      Mushrooms
      Cauliflower
      Vegetable oil
      Seasonings like salt and pepper
  • Directions
      1. Preheat air fryer to 180 C
      2. Mix everything in a bowl
      3. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring after 5 minutes
